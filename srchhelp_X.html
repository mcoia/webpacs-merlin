Search tips for Keyword Search

Use keywords from the title, author, subjects or notes.

Examples:

  • evolution and altruism
  • "achievement test"

For more options, use an Advanced Search.

Search tips for Title begins with

Use this search if you have the title of the book, video, etc. Enter words starting at the beginnning of the title. Exclude initial articles such as "the", "an", and "de" (German).

Examples:

  • Intruder in the Dust
  • Adventures of Huckleberry Finn

Search tips for Journal & Newspaper Title begins with

Search for exact titles for journals, newspapers, or magazines. Enter words starting at the beginning of the title.

Example:

  • journal of management inquiry

If your search is unsuccessful, try a keyword search.

Search Tips for Author People: Type LAST NAME, FIRST NAME Examples:

  • Powell, Lawrence Clark
Corporate Body or Conference name: Type as much of the EXACT NAME as you know. Start at the beginning. Examples:
  • Amnesty International
