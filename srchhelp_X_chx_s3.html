Search tips for Keyword Search

Use keywords from the title, author, subjects or notes.

Examples:

  • evolution and altruism
  • "achievement test"

For more options, use an Advanced Search.

Search tips for Title begins with

Use this search if you have the title of the book, video, etc. Enter words starting at the beginning of the title. Exclude initial articles such as "the", "an", and "de" (German).

Examples:

  • Intruder in the Dust
  • Adventures of Huckleberry Finn

Search Tips for Subjects (Library of Congress)

Search Library of Congress Subject Headings for people, topics, or events.
